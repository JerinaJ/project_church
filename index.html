Welcome to Church of ST.ANTONY

St. Anthony of Padua is one of the Catholic Church’s most popular saints. Saint Anthony of Padua, patron saint of lost and stolen articles, was a powerful Franciscan preacher and teacher.St Anthony is beloved throughout the world and is responsive to all people and all needs. His intercessory powers before our God are awesome.

The objective of website is to GLORIFY THE ALMIGHTY through the service extended at our church St. Antony the worker of million miracles as the patron saint.

St.Antony's church is one of the oldest church in Payamkottai Diocese.The history of the church is starts with 19th century. It is the sub-station parish St.Michael's Church ,Cheranmahadevi..Many people receive miracles from Almighty God through intercession of St.Antony. The church bulit in 1937 by our ancestor.Initially St. Antony’s of Padua Church was in middle of street. Now a Holycross is in the remembrence of old church.Feast of our church held on 2 week of May. We have special prayers on the original 13 days of St.Antony's Feast day (June 13th).On 2013 we celebrated our church's 75th year celebration.Holy God will lead us untill today through intercession of St.Antony.

The church remains a place of peace, hope and communion with God and nature.
